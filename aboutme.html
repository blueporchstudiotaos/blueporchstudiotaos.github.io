About Paul

Exploring the beauty of Taos

I am a full-time artist living and working in Taos, New Mexico. Since visiting New Mexico in my childhood, the New Mexico landscape has inspired my paintings and techniques. I work primarily in oil paints, applied to canvas or wood, and also create assemblages with found objects and woodworking techniques. This website contains a selected portfolio of works over the years. Please contact me to learn more about my work and with any questions.
